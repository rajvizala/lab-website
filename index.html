Welcome to CUBS

The Center for Unified Biometrics and Sensors (CUBS), led by Dr. Nalini K. Ratha, conducts cutting-edge research in biometrics systems security, performance evaluation, and trustworthy AI systems at IBM Thomas J. Watson Research Center.

Research Focus

  • Biometrics systems security
  • Performance evaluation of biometrics systems
  • Trustworthy AI systems
  • Domain adaptation for visual understanding
  • Blockchain in computer vision

Selected Publications

  • "Advances in Biometrics: Sensors, Algorithms and Systems" (Springer)
  • "Automatic Fingerprint Recognition Systems" (Springer)
  • "A Guide to Biometrics Selection and System Design" (Springer)
