Welcome to TRAIL

The Trustworthy AI Lab (TRAIL) is at the forefront of cutting-edge research in trustworthy AI systems, biometrics, and computer vision. Our mission is to develop robust, fair, and secure AI technologies that can be relied upon in critical applications.

At TRAIL, we focus on addressing key challenges in AI trustworthiness, including bias mitigation, fairness in machine learning, and the security of AI systems. Our interdisciplinary team combines expertise in computer science, data science, and ethics to create AI solutions that are not only technologically advanced but also socially responsible.

Our lab collaborates with industry partners and academic institutions worldwide, organizing workshops at prestigious conferences such as CVPR, ECCV, and ICCV. These events cover critical topics like bias in AI, blockchain applications, and advanced biometric technologies.

TRAIL is led by Dr. Nalini K. Ratha, a distinguished researcher in the field of biometrics and AI. Under his guidance, our team of researchers and students works on projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in trustworthy AI and biometric systems.

Key Research Areas

  • Trustworthy AI systems
  • Fairness and bias mitigation in AI
  • Secure and robust biometric systems
  • Computer vision for societal impact
  • Ethical AI and responsible innovation
  • AI in healthcare and security applications

Current Projects

  • Developing bias-aware facial recognition systems
  • Creating explainable AI models for critical decision-making
  • Designing privacy-preserving biometric authentication methods
  • Investigating the use of blockchain in AI transparency
  • Building robust AI systems for healthcare diagnostics
