Research Areas

Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Mining

Artificial Intelligence (AI) researchers now predict that computers will be able to perform tasks that were once considered the prerogative of human beings. Our research in this area focuses on developing advanced AI algorithms, machine learning models, and data mining techniques to solve complex problems across various domains. We explore cutting-edge approaches in deep learning, reinforcement learning, and natural language processing to push the boundaries of what AI can achieve.

Computational Biology and Bioinformatics

This research area focuses on developing highly efficient algorithms and techniques for a wide range of problems arising in smart hospital, smart healthcare, precision medicine, genomics, proteomics, and microarray analysis. Some of the specific problems we address include:

  • Automatic analysis of biomedical images
  • Computer-assisted diagnosis
  • Treatment planning
  • Protein-protein interaction network analysis
  • Protein structure prediction
  • Computational analysis and interpretation of Genomes
  • Evolutionary studies of Genomic ORFans
  • Spatial positioning patterns of the cell nucleus

High-Performance Computing

Our research in High-Performance Computing focuses on developing efficient experimental and theoretical solutions to problems on state-of-the-art computational systems. We work with a variety of advanced computing architectures, including:

  • Clouds
  • Clusters
  • Grids
  • Networks-of-workstations
  • Massively parallel supercomputers
  • GPU-based systems

By leveraging these powerful computational resources, we aim to solve complex problems that require immense processing power and develop scalable algorithms for big data analysis and simulation.
